Dress code

We have a mild preference toward fabulous, but would much prefer you to wear whatever makes you feel comfortable. No nudity at this one, we're afraid.

Behaviour

We would encourage people to be lovely to one another, relax, and enjoy themselves.

The wedding will bring together many of our friends and family, who between them bring a diversity of backgrounds, identities and beliefs. This includes people who are gay, lesbian, bisexual, queer, trans, nonmonogamous, and neurodiverse. Bluntly, lots of our friends are queer, as are we. We trust and expect that all our guests will respect our identities and life choices, and those of our other guests and our other partners.

If you have concerns about this you are welcome to contact us, but we are generally not going to restrict public displays of affection. The day is a celebration of love and commitment, not of exclusivity.
